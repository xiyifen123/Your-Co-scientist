# Your Co-scientist

> **An AI Copilot for Wet Lab Scientists.**
> Focus on discovery. We'll remember the details.

Your Co-scientist is a voice-first AI companion designed for laboratory scientists. It knows where you are in your experiment, reminds you before you make a mistake, and lets you control everything hands-free — because in a wet lab, your hands are always busy.

---

## The Problem

Lab work is painful — not because of the science, but because of the logistics:

| Pain Point | What Happens |
|---|---|
| **Protocol** | Paper protocols get wet, can't be flipped with gloves on, and phone screens require touch |
| **Timer** | Incubation timers are forgotten when you're busy with other experiments |
| **Reminders** | Critical mistakes (missing controls, wrong antibody dilution) are only discovered after results fail |

## Core Features

- **AI Adaptive Workflow** — Generates structured protocols from your experiment plan, with a Critical Review that catches design flaws before you start
- **Voice-First Interaction** — Like a phone call with a reliable colleague. AI speaks, you respond, it advances. No screen touching needed
- **Smart Timer** — Auto-starts countdowns for waiting steps, with voice reminders 5 minutes before completion
- **AI Calculator** — Voice-input dilution ratios and total volumes, get instant calculations
- **Personal Error Library** — Built-in common mistake database + user-customizable reminders ("Always remind me to add PMSF at this step")

## Design Principles

1. **Voice-first, not touch-first** — Hands are always occupied in a wet lab (gloves, pipettes, dishes)
2. **AI is a Reviewer, not an answering machine** — The real value is catching mistakes you didn't think to ask about
3. **Context-aware, not passive** — AI knows which step you're on and proactively advances the workflow
